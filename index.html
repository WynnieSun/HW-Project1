Design Decisions

  • Our theme is cats. There are cats along the navigation bar, in the daily boxes, and a pawprint by each date.
  • We matched the colors from the image of the cat looking out the window to provide a unified color theme.
  • We chose fonts that fit with the colors and images.
  • We adjusted font sizes to make the home page more readable.
  • Hover features also helped readability.
  • We created a navigation bar on the left hand side to make the site easier to navigate.
